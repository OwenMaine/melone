About Us

J Melone & Sons Inc, located at 77 White Pond Rd, Stow, MA, is a trusted provider of ready-mix concrete and construction materials. With years of experience serving the Massachusetts area, we specialize in delivering high-quality concrete products, sand, and gravel solutions for construction projects of all sizes.

Our comprehensive services include building maintenance, dry bulk handling, and construction support, making us your one-stop solution for all concrete and aggregate needs. We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.
